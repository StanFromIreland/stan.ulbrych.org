From the outset, Magda demonstrated a high level of professionalism. She took the time to meet with me for an initial discussion about my child, followed by an extensive interview with both myself and my husband. She then spent considerable time engaging with our son. We were provided with expert guidance, including a Sensory Integration Diet, which offered a range of home-based activities tailored to support his development. My son looks forward to his sessions with ‘Aunt Magda’ every week, and the progress he has made is remarkable. He is now communicating more effectively, follows instructions with greater ease, and has developed a fondness for hugs—something every mother longs for but may not always experience. The clinic is exceptionally well-equipped and centrally located, providing a peaceful environment for parents to relax with a cup of coffee while knowing their child is in the hands of a capable and professional therapist. I highly recommend Magda and her services without hesitation.
