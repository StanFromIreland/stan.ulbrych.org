I've always liked reflecting on the past, for without it, there would be no
present. With help from Maciek (my fellow Polish translation coordinator)
with milestones that predate me, I compiled
a small chart showing [python-docs-pl](https://github.com/python/python-docs-pl)'s
progress since its initial commit, roughly six years ago.

<img src="/translation_progress_en.svg" alt="Translation progress" style="width: 100%; height: auto;" />

The result was quite surprising: I presumed the workshops would align
with the jumps as they introduced new translators to the project, but many remain
unexplained. My raw data is also not ideal, as all the progress calculations are
based on the current branch. As such, it does not show the losses in progress.
It would have simply taken too long otherwise, dealing with dependencies and
building it so many times.

I may return to this in the future.
